Covid-19 info

Vaccinations & testing

Our guests' health and safety is incredibly important to us. We will require that all guests be fully vaccinated: including a booster if you are eligible to receive one in your location On the RSVP, we will ask you to choose whether you would like to text/email a copy of your proof of vaccination or show one of us over a video call. New York City requires proof of vaccination to enter many public spaces, so we also encourage you to bring a copy of your vaccination along with you.

As recent variants have caused breakthrough cases, we also strongly encourage you to take a rapid test 24 hours before the event.

NYC Health and Hospitals provides free Covid testing throughout the city. You can find a testing center on their website. Urgent Care centers also provide testing services (Forest Hills Urgent Care is one close to the venue), however they may not be free. At-home rapid tests are frequently sold out in New York, so we encourage you to not rely on purchasing a test last-minute.

Currently planned precautions

  • The ceremony and cocktail hour will take place outside.
  • The reception is planned to happen indoors, at 25-30% capacity of the venue.
  • The reception area has a large garage door that will be open to provide ventilation.

We may add additional precautionary measures closer to the event date.
