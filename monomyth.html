Heroes' Journey

In true UChicago nerd spirit, we've broken up our story into a highly digestable Joseph Campbell-style monomyth. Here's a little mood-music to enjoy while reading:

The Call to Adventure

Our young heroes meet as fresh-faced UChicago underclassmen in the house lounge of Snell-Hitchcock during Tyler's orientation week.

Refusal of the Call

Despite their many shared interests, Tyler isn't able to get over Nicki's enjoyment of Coldplay. Our heroes remain just friends.

Threshold

As our heroes' mutual friends circle grew, they started hanging out together more and increasingly enjoyed each other's company. They secretly began to wonder: could this become more than just a friendship? Nicki also stopped listening to Coldplay.

Revelation

Tyler and Nicki both participated in the annual UChicago Scavenger Hunt. After four days of intense creation filled with all manners of wondrous eccentricity, our heroes' team won the hunt - and they each other's heart.

Transformation

Years passed, and our heroes have graduated college and are enjoying a leisurely life together in Chicago with their pet roach, Roachbro. But alas - the East Coast beckons with new opportunities. Can our heroes' relationship survive this change in scenery?

Atonement

Our heroes spent two years physically apart, with Nicki attending graduate school in New Haven and Tyler working a new job in New York. Against all odds, however, our heroes' love and longing for each other only grew stronger.

Return

After Nicki graduated from school, our heroes reunited in New York. But wait - first, a throwback vacation in Chicago! And then - a wedding ring! A surprise proposal! And now you are on this website.
